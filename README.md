# Westlake (westlake)
Westlake Corporation is a global Fortune 500 manufacturer and supplier of materials and innovative products that enhance life every day. The company produces chemical materials and building products across multiple sectors including packaging, healthcare, automotive, consumer goods, and construction. Westlake operates through two primary segments: Performance and Essential Materials (PEM) and Housing and Infrastructure Products (HIP), with net sales of approximately $11 billion annually.

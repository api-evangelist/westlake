Westlake Awards Scholarships to Students of Employees Westlake has recognized and rewarded high-achieving college-bound students of our employees for over 30 years. We received an impressive number of submissions this year, all of which were of outstanding quality. Westlake notified 156 students that they are the new recipients for the 2026-2027 Westlake Dependents Scholarship Program.

Jonathan H. Baksht serves as Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Westlake, effective June 2026. His prior experience includes roles at Fortune Brands Innovations, Pactiv Evergreen/Novolex, Valaris Limited, Goldman Sachs, and Andersen Consulting, and he holds degrees from the University of Texas at Austin and Northwestern's Kellogg School of Management.
